  [ftp://ftp.netscape.com/pub/netscape6/japanese/6.2/windows/win32/sea/N6SetupB.exe](ftp://ftp.netscape.com/pub/netscape6/japanese/6.2/windows/win32/sea/N6SetupB.exe)
